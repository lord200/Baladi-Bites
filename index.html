Our Story

Founded in 2018, Baladi Bites emerged from a collective of chefs and sommeliers disillusioned with rigid fine dining traditions. We trade formality for intentionality: every dish is a narrative of seasonality, terroir, and craft.

Our kitchen sources 90% of ingredients from local farms within 100 miles, with a focus on regenerative agriculture and ethical sourcing.
